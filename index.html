Statistical Learning for Large Scale Data
Friday, June 21, 2024
Simon Fraser University, Burnaby, BC, Canada
Home Registration Program Directions Announcements

This conference aims to share the most recent development in applied statistics, focusing on its applications in genomics, pharmaceutical research, and health data science. Invited speakers consist of academic and industry R&D researchers.

Students are encouraged to present their work during poster session 6:00-9:00 on August 15. Travel awards are available for graduate students who present their work at first-come-first-serve basis.
