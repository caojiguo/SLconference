Statistical Learning for Large Scale Data
Friday, June 21, 2024
Simon Fraser University, Burnaby, BC, Canada
Home Registration Program Directions About Richard

The conference is to promote the presentation and discussion on the latest progress in statistical learning for large-scale data and to celebrate the remarkable contributions of Prof. Richard Lockhart in this research field and other areas of statistics. It empowers organizations and researchers to harness the vast amount of information available today, leading to better decision-making, improved efficiency, and the potential for groundbreaking discoveries across various domains.

Postdocs and Students are encouraged to present their work during the poster session. Travel awards are available for postdocs and students who present their work. The best poster award will also be presented at the end of the conference. Please submit your poster title here.
